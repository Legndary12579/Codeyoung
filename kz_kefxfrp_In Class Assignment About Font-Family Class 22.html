How is Sugar Made?

  1. Sugar cane stalks are harvested from nearby fields and then sent to nearby sugar mills.
  2. At the sugar mill, machines grind and press the sugar canes to make sugar cane juice.
  3. The juice is then purified and concentrated on a centrifuge.
  4. The raw sugar gets sent to a cane sugar refinery where it is melted and purified further.
  5. The sugar is then crystalized and transported to grocery stores, for buying.
